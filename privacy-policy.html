Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Flora Dew ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares and protects your personal information when you visit floradew.in or place an order with us.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryExamplesWhen collected
Contact detailsName, phone number, email addressWhen you check out or contact us

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process, pack and ship your order
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates and receipts
  • To respond to questions, complaints or support requests you send us
  • To improve our products, website and customer experience
  • To comply with legal and tax obligations

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Who We Share Information With

  • Razorpay — to securely process your payment. Razorpay handles your card/UPI details directly under its own privacy and security practices; we never see or store your full payment credentials.
  • Courier / logistics partners — your name, phone number and delivery address are shared only to the extent needed to deliver your order.
  • Legal authorities — if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Storage & Security

Order and customer information is stored using reputable cloud infrastructure providers with industry-standard security practices. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain order records for as long as necessary to fulfil orders, handle returns or disputes, and meet accounting/tax obligations, after which they may be securely deleted or anonymised.

6. Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies or local browser storage strictly necessary for core functionality (such as remembering items in your cart during a session). We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your data, where we are not legally required to retain it
  • Withdraw consent for optional communications (e.g. marketing emails, if applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@floradew.in. We aim to respond within a reasonable time, consistent with applicable Indian data protection law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, where it applies to us.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
